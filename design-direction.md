# Portfolio Design Direction

## Palette

* Primary: #0D1B3D — deep navy; anchors heroes, headings, footers, and major sections
* Accent: #16A34A — action green; used sparingly for CTAs, highlights, and emphasis
* Background: #F2F5F9 — cool light-gray canvas, with #FFFFFF for cards and surfaces
* Text: #1E293B — body text; navy #0D1B3D for strong headings, slate #64748B for muted content

## Typography

* Display (headlines): Playfair Display — bold serif headlines with strong editorial presence
* Body: Inter — clean, modern, and optimized for digital interfaces

## Visual language

Flat-blocked + editorial-serif. Large rounded navy slabs floating on a light-gray page, confident solid color blocking with no gradients, patterns, or visual noise. Soft navy-tinted shadows, warm authentic imagery, and clean layouts that balance serif headlines with modern sans-serif body text. Rounded corners remain consistent across cards, sections, and interface elements.

## Tone

Tagline (1 sentence): "Building products, platforms, and experiences that create meaningful impact."

## Voice

Warm-confident. Plain-spoken, approachable, and outcome-focused. Lead with the benefit, explain the solution clearly, and emphasize real-world value over technical complexity. Professional without sounding corporate, modern without sounding trendy.
